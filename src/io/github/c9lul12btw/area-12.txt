> AREA-12 (Biological Research AREA-12)

AREA-12 is a containment and research facility located on remote federal
land in the Midwestern United States. AREA-12 contains both live specimens
of anomalous biological entities as well as samples of biohazardous or otherwise
dangerous biological anomalies for study.

> Objects contained at this facility include: SCP-143, SCP-153, SCP-214, SCP-811, SCP-939
