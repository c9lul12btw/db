> SITE-45 (Research SITE-45)

SITE-45 is a clandestine armed research facility located off the coast of
Western Australia under the Indian Ocean. Here, research and temporary containment
of anomalous objects are conducted under guard and away from a civilian populace.
SITE-45 also serves as a staging point for MTF operations within the Pacific and
Indian Oceans.

> Objects located at the facility include: SCP-321, SCP-2401, SCP-466, SCP-127, SCP-2424, and SCP-1577
