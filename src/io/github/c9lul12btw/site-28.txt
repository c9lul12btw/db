> SITE-28 (Provisional Containment SITE-28)

SITE-28 is located in the SoHo neighborhood of New York City, New York,
United States, and was initially established to contain SCP-602. Since then,
this facility has been expanded into a full-fledged Foundation containment
facility specializing in anomalous artwork and artifacts.
