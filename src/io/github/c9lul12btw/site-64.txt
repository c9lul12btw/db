> SITE-64 (Storage SITE-64)

Located approximately 0.5 km below Forest Park, northwest of Portland,
Oregon, SITE-64 is primarily a low to medium security storage facility,
and is mainly used to house minor anomalous items, and Safe/Euclid class
objects confiscated from the anomalous art community and anomalous electronics
industry based in Portland and Vancouver.

> Objects located at this facility include: SCP-2106, SCP-2306, SCP-2608, SCP-2806, and SCP-2960.
