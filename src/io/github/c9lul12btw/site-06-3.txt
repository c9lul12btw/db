> SITE-06-3 (Humanoid Containment SITE-06-3)

SITE-06-3 is a containment facility that is currently located in the Lorraine
region of France. Formerly located in the United States (SITE-06) and Germany
(SITE-06-2), this facility was moved to its current location following the
decommission and demolition of the aforementioned previous facilities.
SITE-06-3 employs a multi-national staff and houses numerous low-risk human
and humanoid entities, most notably several former Foundation personnel.

> Objects contained at this facility include: SCP-069, SCP-706, SCP-1669, SCP-1702
