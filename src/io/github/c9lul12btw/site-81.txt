> SITE-81 (Research and Containment SITE-81)

Located beneath Lake Monroe in Bloomington, Indiana, SITE-81 is the primary
hub for anomalous activity in the United States Midwest. Initially founded
as an expeditionary SITE to monitor SCP-2812, SITE-81 has since grown to become
one of the largest SITEs in the region. Located within is the Foundation's
Classifications Department, as well as a massive Mobile Task Force deployment
outpost.

> Objects contained at this SITE include: SCP-715, SCP-2080, SCP-2151, SCP-2270, SCP-2445, SCP-2496, SCP-2540, SCP-2564, SCP-2996.
